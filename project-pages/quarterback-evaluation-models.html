Predicting NCAA Quarterback Success in the National Football League

View on GitHub

Description

Every year, quarterback prospects are touted as generational talents before the NFL draft. Most of these prospects, however, do not pan out in the NFL, costing teams a high draft pick, and leaving them once again in search of a new quarterback. This project aimed to leverage machine learning to predict how well a quarterback would transition into the NFL by classifying them into one of 6 tiers, ranging from practice squad to hall of famer. The model created provided reliable predictions, as it had over 70 percent accuracy.

Key Features

  • Classifier model for NCAA quarterbacks to NFL tiers
  • Clustering model for current and recent NFL quarterbacks to sort them into tiers
  • Web scraper to scrape Quarterback statistics and data from many online source
